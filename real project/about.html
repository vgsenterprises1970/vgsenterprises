VGS Enterprises - About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2016, VGS Enterprises has been at the forefront of the Adhesives industry, offering high-quality bonding solutions for a variety of sectors. VGS Enterprises is an authorized distributor of 3M India LTD. Our mission is to provide products that deliver lasting performance and reliability, helping businesses meet their adhesive needs with confidence.

Our Values

At VGS Enterprises, we are committed to:

  • Quality: We ensure the highest standards in every product.
  • Innovation: We continuously improve our products to meet the changing demands of the market.
  • Customer-Centric: We focus on understanding our customers needs to deliver tailored solutions.
  • Reliability: Emphasizing the trust, customers can place in our adhesives and tapes for consistent and lasting performance.
  • High Performance: Showing that our products are designed to meet rigorous industry standards.

Our Mission

We aim to be the leading provider of adhesive and tapes solutions, helping industries grow by offering reliable, high-performance bonding products. Our goal is to support our customers with innovative products that make their operations more efficient and effective.
